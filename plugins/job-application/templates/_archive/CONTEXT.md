# _archive — Your past applications (read-only mine)

**Layer 3 (reference).** Drop your **previous CVs and cover letters** here.

## Why
The pipeline mines this folder for your real phrasing, achievement bullets, and voice, then writes
them into `00_master/experience-bank.md` and `voice.md`. The better your archive, the more the
tailoring sounds like you.

## How
1. Copy old application files here (any format; `.md`, `.pdf`, `.docx`, `.txt`).
2. Ask the pipeline to mine them, or run `/job-application:job-setup --mine-archive`.

## Rules
- Read-only for the pipeline once mined — it pulls phrasing, it does not rewrite your history.
- Nothing here is submitted; it's raw material.
