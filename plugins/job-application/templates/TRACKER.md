# Application Tracker

Deeper per-application notes that don't fit on the Kanban card. The board (`Applications Board.md`)
is the quick view; this is the long memory.

## How to use
- The board holds the status (which lane a company is in).
- Add a section here only when an application needs more than a card: research notes, contact
  history, interview prep, follow-up dates.
