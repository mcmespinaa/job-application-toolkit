## Structure (proven pattern — keep or adapt)
1. **Opening** — name the exact role + a one-line hook on why it fits.
2. **Experience match** — 2-3 sentences mapping past work directly to the job ad's requirements.
3. **Key responsibilities / competencies** — a short bulleted list from experience-bank.md, matched to the ad.
4. **Personal qualities** — one line of self-description (see your voice phrases below).
5. **Company connection** — name something specific you value about THIS employer.
6. **Close** — thank them, invite a conversation, repeat contact details (email + phone).

## What to avoid
- No invented achievements, metrics, or employers not in master-cv.md / experience-bank.md.
- No generic openers — always name the role and a reason.
- Don't repeat the CV verbatim; the letter argues the *fit*, the CV lists the *facts*.
- Keep to one page (~350-450 words).
