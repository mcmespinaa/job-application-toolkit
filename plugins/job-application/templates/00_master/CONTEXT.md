# 00_master — The Factory (source of truth)

**Layer 3 (reference).** The ONLY place your real facts live. Everything downstream reads from here.

## Files
- `master-cv.md` — your contact, profile, work history, education, skills. Fill every [FILL IN].
- `experience-bank.md` — reusable bullets + angles, built from your `_archive/` past applications.
- `voice.md` — how your cover letters sound.

## Rules
- Edit these freely when your real experience changes.
- The pipeline NEVER edits these while drafting an application — they're upstream.
- Never write anything here you can't honestly stand behind. No invented metrics, titles, or employers.
